## 🎨 **COMPLETE WORKFLOW:**

2. **Room Detail** opens
3. Enter dimensions: L=15, W=12, H=8
4. Enter prep hours: 2
5. **See surface cards below** (all empty/white)
6. Click "Walls" card
7. Modal opens with auto-calculated 432 sq ft
8. Fill product/sheen/color/coats → Save
